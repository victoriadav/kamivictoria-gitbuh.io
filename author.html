Harper Lee's Background:

Harper Lee was born on April 28, 1926 in Monroeville, Alabama. She was the youngest of four children. Most of the inspiration for her Pulitzer Award winning novel "To Kill a Mockingbird" came from her family and her childhood. Maycomb is a fictional city based on Monroeville (where she grew up). Her father was a lawyer who also served in the state legislature which inspired Atticu's character. It's safe to say Lee portrayed her roots throughout the novel. She passed away on February 19, 2016.
